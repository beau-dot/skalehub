The Real Problem

The leak isn't in your ads. It's what they drive to.

Most companies running paid ads send cold traffic to a homepage built for everyone — or a generic "Contact Us" form that converts almost no one. The prospect that just clicked your $12 CPM ad bounces in 3 seconds.

You can't fix a conversion problem by spending more on traffic. You fix it by building the right system for what happens after the click.

"The problem almost never lives inside the ad itself. It lives in what happens after the click."
1
Sending Traffic to Your Homepage

Bounce rates above 70%. Your homepage is built for everyone — cold paid traffic needs one specific reason to convert.

2
Offers That Aren't Worth the Friction

"Sign up for our newsletter" is not an offer. Cold traffic won't hand over their email for nothing of value.

3
Zero Segmentation Before the Sales Call

Every lead gets the same follow-up. Tire-kickers and $50K opportunities treated identically.

4
Generic (Or Nonexistent) Follow-Up

Same 3-email drip to everyone, regardless of what they told you. CRM full of dead leads.

5
Measuring Clicks Instead of Pipeline

CTR: 2.3%, CPM: $12, Impressions: 45,000. Pipeline: empty. You're optimizing the wrong metric.
